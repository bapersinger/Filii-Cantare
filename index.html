About Us

Filii Cantare was founded in 2015 to give children in the North Houston area the opportunity to learn music and have fun. Each season, our students give 2 concerts where we partner with other local charities. Our hope is that your child will not only hone their musical craft, but also learn valuable lessons about community and philanthropy.

Some of the local charities we have parterned with are Coreluv and No Kill Houston. We are a 501(c)(3) Charity and as such, all donations are tax-deductible. Please contact us if you are interested in sponsoring one of our students.
